Power Ups

OutZone features a lot of power ups that make the game more interesting

Power Up
This power up makes your weapon stronger
but if you die you lose the charges
Change
Swaps your weapon between 3 shoot mode and aiming mode
Bomb
Gives you an air strike which can destroy almost every enemy in the game
Energy
Recovers the player's energy
