OutZone

While it is true that Out Zone has a story it was only available in the Japanese version. The plot itself is quite simple, it's the year 2097 and after several invasions from aliens the Earth is deperate and nearly giving away it's last breath. Then the forces of Earth in a desperate try to save their lives and their nation, they send their last secret weapon, cyborg super soldiers controlled by the player. They deploy our cyborg heroes in a waste ground in the planet where the alien headquarters are, from there our heros will have to keep running and killing nearly every enemy on sight to fullfil their purpourse to save the Earth.
